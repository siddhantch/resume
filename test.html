OSCILLATORY TURING PATTERNS IN REACTION-DIFFUSION SYSTEMS

Jan'21 - April'21

Objective
  • To replicate the results of a research paper titled - Oscillatory Turing Patterns in Reaction-Diffusion Systems with Two Coupled Layer by Lingfa Yang and Irving R. Epstein
Approach
  • Simulated the proposed model in MATLAB using finite difference method to obtain 4 different Oscillatory Turing patterns corresponding to different parameters that were observed in the paper.
  • Obtained the Real and Imaginary dispersion curves of the eigenvalues of each pattern.
  • Obtained the bifurcation graph of an isolated half system which gave insight into the dependance of the nature of the patterns on the parameters pertaining to the system.
Result
  • Successfully replicated all the patterns observed in the paper with their corresponding dispersion graphs.
  • Confirmed the finding of the paper that the observed patterns were a consequence of the interaction between a wave instability and a Turing instability.
